abstract: >
  We develop an integrated model for airline scheduling, fleet assignment, and
  pricing in a monopolized market. The model explicitly captures supply-demand
  interactions through a discrete choice passenger demand model and is solved
  using a local search heuristic combined with column generation. Results show
  significant revenue improvements over hierarchical approaches.
