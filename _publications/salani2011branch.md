abstract: >
  We develop a branch-and-price algorithm for the vehicle routing problem with
  discrete split deliveries and time windows. The pricing subproblem is solved
  via dynamic programming, and branching strategies are designed to handle the
  combinatorial structure of split deliveries. Computational results demonstrate
  effectiveness on benchmark instances.
