abstract: >
  This paper addresses the tactical berth allocation problem (TBAP) at container
  terminals, which involves assigning vessels to berths over a planning horizon.
  We propose mixed-integer programming models and solution methods that integrate
  quay crane assignment and minimize transshipment-related costs.
