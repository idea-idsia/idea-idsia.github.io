abstract: >
  We propose decremental state space relaxation strategies and initialization
  heuristics for the orienteering problem with time windows. The approach
  progressively tightens relaxations until feasibility is achieved, and
  leverages dynamic programming for exact optimization. Computational results
  demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed strategies on benchmark instances.
