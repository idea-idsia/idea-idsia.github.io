## Overview

**GOLIA (Governing, Optimising and Leveraging Innovations proActively)** is a Horizon Europe project that aims to transform how urban mobility systems are planned, governed, and delivered across Europe.

The project develops an **Adaptive Mobility Smart Governance Model** that integrates:

- Data-driven decision making  
- Social and environmental considerations  
- Inclusive stakeholder participation  

Its overarching goal is to create **accessible, equitable, and resilient mobility systems** aligned with the **European Green Deal and Sustainable Development Goals**

---

## Motivation

Urban mobility systems today face several structural challenges:

- Fragmented governance across institutional levels  
- Limited integration of data and digital tools  
- Insufficient inclusion of citizens in decision-making  
- Persistent car dependency and social inequalities  

GOLIA addresses these gaps by shifting mobility planning from purely economic optimisation toward a **“social optimum” approach**, incorporating factors such as:

- Health and quality of life  
- Accessibility and equity  
- Environmental impact
